Night at the Museum

Get ready for an unforgettable adventure — a Night at the Museum like never before!

Pack your sleeping bag, grab your flashlight, and bring the whole family for an exciting overnight experience surrounded by towering dinosaurs, fascinating science exhibits, and hands-on fun.

But the adventure doesn't stop there — you'll dive into exciting science experiments, build and test your own creations, and get your hands messy with interactive activities that spark curiosity. Whether you're a budding paleontologist, a junior chemist, or just love exploring, there's something here for everyone.

As the last visitors leave and the lights go down, the real magic begins. You'll join us for exclusive after-hours flashlight tours through dimly lit halls where shadows dance and secrets await discovery. Tiptoe past ancient fossils, peer into mysterious displays, and listen as our guides reveal stories hidden in every corner of the museum.

When it's finally time to roll out your sleeping bag, you'll drift off under the watchful eyes of dinosaurs or beside glowing exhibits, surrounded by the quiet wonder of a museum transformed by night.
Don't miss your chance to create lifelong memories at this one-of-a-kind overnight event — filled with discovery, laughter, and wonder.

Whether you're stargazing in the planetarium, building your own rocket, or listening to space stories under the dim glow of the moon, this is your chance to experience the museum in a whole new way — full of wonder, discovery, and excitement.

Kjelsåsveien 143, 0491 Oslo

Opening Hours

Monday-Friday: 10:00-16:00

Saturday: 11:00-15:00

Sunday: 12:00-15:00

Admission

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you've left the museum.
