Kids

Come visit our museum — it's like stepping into a giant science playground! You can launch mini-rockets, explore cool robots, make electricity with your own hands, and even walk through a human-sized heart. There are games, puzzles, experiments, and tons of fun facts waiting around every corner. Whether you're a future astronaut, engineer, or just love to tinker and explore, there's loads to do and learn. So grab your curiosity, bring your friends or family, and get ready for a science adventure you won't forget!

Teachers

As a community-driven museum, we are passionate about partnering with schools to create dynamic, hands-on learning experiences that go beyond the classroom. We believe museums are more than places to observe — they are places to interact, explore, and imagine. Our dedicated education team offers guided tours tailored to your curriculum, helping students connect what they learn in school with the real-world wonders on display.

Researchers

Are you eager to collaborate with our team of researchers and academics? At our museum, we welcome individuals and institutions who are passionate about discovery, innovation, and knowledge-sharing. We offer a range of opportunities for you to engage with our research initiatives, access our collections, and contribute to ongoing projects. Whether you're an independent scholar, a postgraduate student, or part of a university department, you'll find valuable resources here — including comprehensive online archives, access to our laboratory facilities, and curated collections ready for in-depth study.

Admission

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you've left the museum.
