Welcome to our new experience center!

Come explore the amazing world of science at the Community Science Museum! Whether you're young or old, there's something awesome waiting just for you.

Control real robots, play coding games, and explore how technology is shaping the future. It's like entering a sci-fi movie — but it's all real!

With new exhibits added all the time, every visit is a new adventure. So bring your curiosity, bring your questions, and get ready to discover the fun side of science! The future is waiting — are you ready to explore it?

Uncover bones like a real paleontologist and stand face-to-face with life-sized dinosaurs. Can you tell a raptor from a T-Rex?

Get messy (in a good way!) making slime, testing gravity, or creating colorful chemical reactions. Our interactive lab zones let YOU be the scientist!

Test your brain with cool puzzles, games, and hands-on experiments.

But that's just the beginning. Step into our planetarium and take a journey through the stars — fly past planets, witness a supernova, and learn what makes black holes so mysterious.

Admission

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you've left the museum.
