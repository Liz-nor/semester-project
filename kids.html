Welcome to our new experience center!

Come explore the amazing world of science at the Community Science Museum! Whether you're young or old, there's something awesome waiting just for you.

Control real robots, play coding games, and explore how technology is shaping the future. It's like entering a sci-fi movie — but it's all real!

Test your brain with cool puzzles, games, and hands-on experiments.

Uncover bones like a real paleontologist and stand face-to-face with life-sized dinosaurs. Can you tell a raptor from a T-Rex?

Get messy (in a good way!) making slime, testing gravity, or creating colorful chemical reactions. Our interactive lab zones let YOU be the scientist!
